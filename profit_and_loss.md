### Q1.

A shopkeeper bought an item for ₹500 and sold it for ₹600. What is the profit percentage?

A) 15%
B) 20%
C) 25%
D) 30%

**Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
Cost Price (CP) = ₹500
Selling Price (SP) = ₹600
Profit = SP - CP = 600 - 500 = ₹100
Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100 = (100 / 500) × 100 = **20%**

### Q149.

A customer gets a discount of 25% on the marked price of an article. The shopkeeper still makes a profit of 50%. If the cost price is ₹720, what is the marked price of the article?

A) ₹1280
B) ₹1340
C) ₹1400
D) ₹1440

**Answer:** D

**Explanation:**
CP = ₹720, Profit = 50%
SP = 720 × 1.50 = ₹1080
Discount = 25%
SP = 75% of MP
1080 = 0.75 × MP
MP = 1080/0.75 = **₹1440**
