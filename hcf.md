
### Q1.

Find the HCF of 180 and 252.

A) 18
B) 24
C) 36
D) 72

**Answer:** C

**Explanation:**
180 = 2² × 3² × 5
252 = 2² × 3² × 7
HCF = 2² × 3² = **36**.
