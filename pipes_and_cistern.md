### Q149.

A tank has two pipes. The first pipe can fill it in 45 minutes and the second can empty it in 1 hour. If two pipes be opened alternately for 1 minute each, starting with filling pipe, then the tank will be filled in:

A) 5 hrs 53 min
B) 5 hrs 55 min
C) 5 hrs 40 min
D) 6 hrs

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
Fill rate = 1/45. Empty rate = 1/60.
2 min work = 1/45 - 1/60 = (4-3)/180 = 1/180.
Work to do before last step: Tank full - 1/45 = 1 - 4/180 = 176/180.
Pairs needed: 176 pairs.
Time = 176 * 2 = 352 min.
Filled = 176/180.
Remaining = 4/180 = 1/45.
Next min filling pipe works and fills 1/45.
Total time = 352 + 1 = 353 min.
353 min = 5 hours 53 min.
Answer = **5 hrs 53 min**
