# Logical Reasoning - Number Series

### Q1.

7, 10, 8, 11, 9, 12, ... What number should come next?

A) 10
B) 7
C) 13
D) 15

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
This is an alternating addition and subtraction series. 7+3=10, 10-2=8, 8+3=11, 11-2=9, 9+3=12, 12-2=10.
