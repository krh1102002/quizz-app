# Logical Reasoning - Statement and Assumption MCQs

### Q1.
**Problem:** 
**Statement:** "If you are a computer engineer, we have a challenging job for you." - An advertisement by a company.
**Assumptions:**
I. Computer engineers are available in the market.
II. The company requires computer engineers.
III. The job offered by the company is challenging.

A) Only I and II are implicit
B) Only II and III are implicit
C) Only I and III are implicit
D) All I, II and III are implicit

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:**
1. Assumption I is implicit because an advertisement is placed only when it is assumed that potential candidates exist.
2. Assumption II is implicit as the company is specifically calling for computer engineers.
3. Assumption III is implicit because the advertisement explicitly states the job is "challenging."

### Q4.
**Problem:** 
**Statement:** "If you want to buy a luxury car, X is the best choice." - An advertisement.
**Assumptions:**
I. People want to buy luxury cars.
II. There are other luxury cars available in the market.
III. X is indeed the best among all luxury cars.

A) Only I and II are implicit
B) Only II and III are implicit
C) Only I and III are implicit
D) All I, II and III are implicit

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:**
1. Advertisement assumes there is a market for the product (I).
2. "Best" implies a comparison with existing alternatives (II).
3. Assumption III is a claim, not an assumption. A person reading the ad might not assume it's true, but the advertiser assumes it to be their selling point. However, in logical assumption, we look for what MUST be true for the statement to make sense.

### Q5.
**Problem:** 
**Statement:** "The government has decided to increase the price of petroleum products from next week."
**Assumptions:**
I. People may protest against the decision.
II. The government will be able to manage the protests.
III. The increase in price will reduce the consumption of petroleum.

A) Only I and II are implicit
B) Only II and III are implicit
C) Only I and III are implicit
D) None of these

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:** 
Assumptions are things taken for granted. Protests are a possible reaction (I), and managing them is a necessity (II), but neither is an underlying assumption of the decision itself unless specified. Consumption reduction (III) might be an objective, but not an assumption.
