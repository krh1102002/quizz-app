B) 200
C) 150
D) 250

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

---

### Q2.

What is the central angle corresponding to Drawing in the pie chart?

A) 90°
B) 72°
C) 100°
D) 108°

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
25% of 360° = (25/100) * 360 = 90°.

**Explanation:**
