## Set 1: Production of Steel by 5 Companies (in Million Tonnes)

| Company | 2021 | 2022 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Company A | 45 | 50 |
| Company B | 30 | 35 |
| Company C | 60 | 55 |
| Company D | 40 | 45 |
| Company E | 25 | 30 |

### Q1.

What is the average production of steel by all companies in 2022?

A) 43 Million Tonnes
B) 40 Million Tonnes
C) 45 Million Tonnes
D) 42 Million Tonnes

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
Average (2022) = (50+35+55+45+30) / 5 = 215 / 5 = 43.

### Q2.

A) Company C
B) Company A
C) Company B
D) Company D

**Answer:** A

### Q3.

What is the ratio of production of Company B in 2021 to Company D in 2022?

A) 2 : 3
B) 3 : 4
C) 4 : 5
D) 1 : 2

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
30 : 45 = 2 : 3.

---

### Q4.

Total production of Company A and E in 2021 is what percent of total production of Company C and D in 2022?

A) 70%
B) 75%
C) 80%
D) 65%

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
A+E (2021) = 45+25 = 70.
C+D (2022) = 55+45 = 100.
% = 70/100 = 70%.

---

### Q5.

What is the percentage increase in total production of all companies from 2021 to 2022?

A) 7.5%
B) 10%
C) 5%
D) 12%

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
Total 2021 = 45+30+60+40+25 = 200.
Total 2022 = 215.
Increase = 15. % Increase = 15/200 * 100 = 7.5%.
