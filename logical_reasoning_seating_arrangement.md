### Q1.

**Problem:** A, B, C, D, and E are sitting on a bench. A is sitting next to B, C is sitting next to D, D is not sitting with E who is on the left end of the bench. C is on the second position from the right. A is to the right of B and E. A and C are sitting together. In which position is A sitting?

A) Between B and D
B) Between B and C
C) Between E and D
D) Between C and E

**Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
1. E is at the left end: E _ _ _ _
2. C is second from the right: E _ _ C _
3. C is next to D: D cannot be at the end because E is not with D. So D must be at the right end. E _ _ C D
4. A and C are together: E _ A C D
5. A is next to B: E B A C D
Final arrangement: E, B, A, C, D.
A is between B and C.
