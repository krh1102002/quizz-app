# Logical Reasoning - Blood Relation MCQs

### Q1.
**Problem:** Pointing towards a girl, Anurag says, "This girl is the daughter of only child of my father." What is the relation of Anurag's wife with that girl?
A) Mother
B) Sister
C) Aunt
D) Daughter

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:**
1. "Only child of my father" means Anurag himself.
2. "The girl is the daughter of only child of my father" means the girl is Anurag's daughter.
3. Therefore, Anurag's wife is the girl's mother.
---

### Q2.
**Problem:** If A + B means A is the daughter of B; A - B means A is the husband of B; A * B means A is the brother of B. From the expression P + Q - R, which of the following is true?
A) R is the mother of P
B) R is the sister of P
C) R is the aunt of P
D) R is the mother-in-law of P

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:**
1. P + Q means P is the daughter of Q.
2. Q - R means Q is the husband of R.
3. Since Q is the husband of R, R is the wife of Q.
4. Since P is the daughter of Q, and R is Q's wife, R is the mother of P.
---
