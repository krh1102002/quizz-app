# Logical Reasoning - Clocks MCQs

### Q1.
**Problem:** What is the angle between the hour hand and the minute hand of a clock when the time is 3:25?
A) 47.5 degrees
/B) 47 degrees
C) 46.5 degrees
D) 48 degrees

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:**
1. Angle by hour hand in 12 hours = 360°.
2. Angle by hour hand in 3 hours 25 minutes (3 + 25/60 hours) = (360/12) * (3 + 5/12) = 30 * (41/12) = 102.5°.
3. Angle by minute hand in 60 minutes = 360°.
4. Angle by minute hand in 25 minutes = (360/60) * 25 = 6 * 25 = 150°.
5. Angle between them = |150° - 102.5°| = 47.5°.

### Q2.
**Problem:** At what time between 4 and 5 o'clock will the hands of a watch be together?
A) 21 9/11 min past 4
B) 21 8/11 min past 4
C) 20 min past 4
D) 22 min past 4

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:**
1. At 4 o'clock, the minute hand is 20 minute spaces behind the hour hand.
2. To be together, the minute hand must gain 20 minute spaces.
3. 55 minute spaces are gained in 60 minutes.
4. 20 minute spaces are gained in (60/55) * 20 = (12/11) * 20 = 240/11 = 21 9/11 minutes.

### Q10.
**Problem:** A watch which gains 5 seconds in 3 minutes was set right at 7 a.m. In the afternoon of the same day, when the watch indicated quarter past 4 o'clock, the true time is:

**Explanation:**

7. 7 a.m. + 9 hours = 4 p.m.
