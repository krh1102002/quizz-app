### Q1.

The average of 5 numbers is 24. If one number is excluded, the average becomes 22. Find the excluded number.

A) 28
B) 30
C) 32
D) 34

**Answer:** C

**Explanation:**
Sum of 5 numbers = 5 × 24 = 120
Sum of remaining 4 numbers = 4 × 22 = 88
Excluded number = 120 − 88 = **32**.
